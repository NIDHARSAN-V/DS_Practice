OUTLIER PROBLEM

🚨 Why you got many outliers (623)

👉 Your data is likely:

Right-skewed (long tail)

Meaning:

Most patients stay short
Few stay very long

👉 IQR struggles with skewed data

✅ What should YOU do?
✔ Option 1 (Best for ML) → KEEP them
Do nothing ✔
✔ Option 2 → Capping (recommended)
Q1 = df['length_of_stay'].quantile(0.25)
Q3 = df['length_of_stay'].quantile(0.75)
IQR = Q3 - Q1

upper = Q3 + 1.5 * IQR

df['length_of_stay'] = df['length_of_stay'].clip(upper=upper)

👉 Keeps data but limits extreme effect

✔ Option 3 → Log transformation (VERY powerful)
import numpy as np
df['length_of_stay_log'] = np.log1p(df['length_of_stay'])

👉 Fixes skewness instead of deleting data

❌ Option to AVOID (in your case)
Removing all 623 rows ❌

👉 Why bad?

You lose critical patient cases
Model becomes biased toward “normal” patients
